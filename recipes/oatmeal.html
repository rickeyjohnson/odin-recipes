Oatmeal

Description

This recipe is my grandma's famous cinnamon and brown sugar oatmeal recipe for anyone who needs a light snack, a quick breakfast, or a hearty meal. Serving size is 2 people, best served warm.

Ingredients

  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Water
  • Oats

Steps

  1. Boil 1 3/4 cups of water in a pot
  2. When water is boiling add a spoonful of vanilla extract
  3. Add 1 cup of oats to the water
  4. Set heat on medium-low and stir
  5. Add 4 - 6 good shakes of cinnamon, stir
  6. Add a spoonful of butter, stir
  7. Add 2 good scoops of brown sugar, THE MORE THE BETTER
  8. Stir and cook 1 min over medium-low heat then simmer to off and let sit for 10 minutes

Done!
